Main method of the process.

When watchdog exec()s the process, process starts from here as expected. It expects 2 arguments namely: id and output_file. It also maps the signals with corresponding handler methods. Writes to output_file that it was born, then sleeps until receiving a signal. If it cant locate the output_file, then it terminates with error.
